Juggernog

Juggernog is one of the first 4 perks introduced to zombies, it has appeared in every COD:Zombies game since World At War,, it boost your health so that you can take more hits. just like the other perk machines, Juggernog has a jingle "When you need some help to get by, something to make you feel strong. Reach for Juggernog tonight, sugar seduction delight! When you need to feel big and strong, reach for Juggernog tonight!"
