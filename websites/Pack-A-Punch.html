Pack-A-Punch

the Pack-A-Punch machine was made by Jebidiah brown in 1885, keepers had given him a vision and told him how to build it. the Pack-a-Punch Machine is found in every Zombies map after Shi No Numa except Dead Ops Arcade, Bus Depot (Survival), Diner, and Farm), the Pack-A-Punch has different versions, such as the Ubersprengen in COD:WW11 and the Upgrade Station in COD:Advanced Warfare.
